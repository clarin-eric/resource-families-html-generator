The CLASSLA-StanfordNLP model for morphosyntactic annotation of standard Bulgarian 1.1

Annotation: morphosyntax
Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0

Bulgarian

This model for morphosyntactic annotation of standard Bulgarian was built with CLASSLA-StanfordNLP tool by training on the BulTreeBank training corpus and using the CoNLL2017 word embeddings. The model simultaneously produces UPOS, FEATS and XPOS (MULTEXT-East) labels. The estimated F1 of the XPOS annotations is ~96.8.

The model is available for download from the CLARIN.SI repository.

For the relevant publication, see Ljubešić and Dobrovoljc (2019)

Annotation: morphosyntax
Licence: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0

Czech
